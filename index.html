Saeedeh Komijani

Graduate Student Researcher · Computer Science, UC Davis

I am interested in advancing data-driven approaches that bridge neuroscience, health, and education. In collaboration with the UC Davis MIND Institute, I analyze multimodal data—including fMRI, VR eye-tracking, and behavioral rating scales—from children, youth, and adults. By developing machine-learning models on these diverse datasets, I aim to uncover patterns that deepen our understanding of attention, impulsivity, and related processes. The broader goal of this work is to translate scientific insights into practical strategies that support both clinical care and educational innovation.
